# Kampus App

Kampus is a **social campus mobile application** designed to enhance student life by
connecting students, clubs, and university administration through a unified digital
platform. The application enables students to discover campus communities, participate
in events, engage in discussions, and build meaningful academic and social connections.

- Students can interact socially before, during, and after events

Kampus aims to transform campus life into a more **interactive, transparent, and
